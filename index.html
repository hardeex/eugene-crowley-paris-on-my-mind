Author's Background

Eugene Crowley holds a master's degree in general psychology from Roosevelt University, Chicago, and was a member of the Jungian Institute in Evanston, Illinois. His rich life experiences include extensive travels throughout Europe and a transformative journey to South America in 2002. In 2009, Mr. Crowley published Upside Down World: The Loss of the Sacred Cosmos, showcasing his deep intellectual engagement.
